Dean and Krista woke up early with a big plan to plant a beautiful garden. They were excited to turn their backyard into a lovely oasis.

Poppy sensed their sadness. She zoomed around the yard in her cape, wagging her tail and barking happily. Her joy was contagious.

Watching Poppy play, Dean and Krista smiled and felt their spirits lift. They remembered how much fun they were having together. They decided to keep going.

With renewed energy, they put the finishing touches on their garden. It was hard work, but they felt proud of what they had accomplished together.
